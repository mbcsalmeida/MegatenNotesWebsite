## Table of Contents

## Chapter 1

Stats:
- All STR

Pick Pixie.

Go to hospital room (bottom), exchange MAG, exchange 3000 MAG for money.

Go to shop (second), buy starting from the bottom:
- 20k sword
- Dullahan Helm
- Survival Vest
- 5x Medicine
- 2 Dis-stones
- 2 Dis-paralyzes
- 6x Hell Beads

Equip them and leave. Go to floor 32 and grind to level 9. Recruit a Knocker, Jack Frost and Jack Lantern.

Keep grinding but head to floor 31. Summon Jack Frost and Jack Lantern. Fight the 3 Freia:
- Hell Bead/Defend, Mabufu, Heal Bead
- Might take a couple of times.
- 2 into VIT, rest STR
- Any demon points go into VIT

Hang around grinding for money for the shop below.

Sell Mag. Make sure to have (needs 22600):
- 3 Mabufu Stones
- 5 Dis-paralyzes
- 3x Stingers

Grind to 14 and put points into VIT. Summon Jack Frost and Pyro Jack in front of the Lilims:
- If you get charmed, put to sleep first turn or hit with too many bufulas, it's over. Which is most of the time.
- Stinger, Stinger, Stinger
- After using Stingers, switch to Mabufu Stones, hopefully you'll freeze.
- Once there's one left, you can use Hell Beads if there are any left.
- Prioritze items over magic
- The miss rate is insane, might take a couple of tries
- All into STR

Grind until level 18 either on floor 26 or floor 24. Then go to the mansion:
- Pixie x Jack Frost = Rusalka
Summon another Pixie (50 MAG).
- Pixie x Knocker = Azumi

Now we have to slowly grind to level 25. Summon Rusalka which helps you heal and escape from battles. Don't let her die.
In the meantime, grind enough money to buy more armor, buy as soon as possible (8100):
- Dark Crown (3600)
- Cyber Arm (4500)

Fuse:
- Azumi x Rusalka (60 MAG) = Apsaras

Grind money, enough to buy:
- Make sure to have around 9 Hell Beads
- 2x Charms
- 2x Kindan

You also want to leave Chapter 1 with aroudn 3000 Macca at least. Head to Floor 22 with Apsaras in the party to grind more for levels. Once you reach level 23, head back up to recruit a Werecat.

Fuse:
- Werecat x Pixie (50MAG) = Momunofu (70MAG)

Good Demons to fight:
- Sylph/Baykok - Have magic, dangerous. Singled out can win.
- Wendigo - Weak to Maragi
- Kushinada-Hime/Berith(Agilao) - Fightable
- Atsu - Super weak to elec

Bad Demons to fight:
- Worm - Bind
- Yakshni - Charms and has Diarama...
- Undyne - Diarahan...
- Lilims - Charm...

At 22, recruit Hakutaku for Trafuri.

Once at level 25, recruit a Sylph.

Floor 24 - down left up right
Floor 23 - down right up left down right middle up
Floor 22 - up left up right down
Floor 21 - right up all the way left down right

Formation: Hero, Apsaras, Rusalka. Fight Kirin:
- Charm, Rakukaja, Charm.
- Hell Bead, Hell Bead, Defend.
- Once there's one left, you can attack with sword and hopefully heal with Diarama.

## Chapter 2

Now we need to grind once more, yay. Sell the sword to buy: 
- Dragon Boots - 7000

We need to grind to be able to afford 5(10?) Stingers.
Best initial grind is on floor 19 in front of the door and try to stun enemies with Apsaras and Sylph. Then go to floor 17.
Demons:
- Purshki - Very weak to elec, good for pixie strat if singled out
- Papilsag - Not really weak to much, has tetrakan
- Badb Catha - Has panic voice, annoying
- Shitori - Has Maragion, "weak" to phys

Floor 18
- Archangel - Pretty easy to defeat with phys
- everyone else sucks

Floor 17
- Orthrus - weak to elec
- Salamander - weak to ice
- Sarasvati - tetrakarn and makarakarn...

Floor 19 - down right - up - right, down - left
Floor 18 - down right all way up - all left - all down - right up
Floor 17 - left down - right down - up right - down left - right all up after cutscene
Floor 16 - down right up left down
