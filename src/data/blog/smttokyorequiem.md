## General

Stats:
- All STR

Pick Pixie.

Go to hospital room (bottom), exchange MAG, exchange 3000 MAG for money.

Go to shop (second), buy starting from the bottom:
- 20k sword
- Geburah Vest
- Combat Boots
- 10x Medicine
- 2 Dis-stones
- 2 Dis-paralyzes
- 7x Hell Beads

Equip them and leave. Go to floor 32 and grind to level 8. Recruit a Jack Frost.

Keep grinding but head to floor 31. Summon Jack Frost and Pixie. Fight the 3 Freia:
- Hell Bead, Mabufu, Heal Bead
- Might take a couple of times.
- Any demon points go into VIT

Hang around and recruit a Pyro Jack.

Sell Mag. Make sure to have:
- 4-5 Mabufu Stones
- At least 4 Hell Beads, the more the better

Grind to 14 and put points into VIT. Summon Jack Frost and Pixie in front of the Lilims:
- If you get charmed, put to sleep first turn or hit with too many bufulas, it's over. Which is most of the time.
