### These notes are for the Super Famicom version of Shin Megami Tensei 1. They use an exploit that does not work in the PS1 or GBA versions. Runs of this game can be done with the English Patch (without QoL improvements) or the JP version. The English Patched version is confirmed to be considerably slower than the JP version so keep it in mind. All the names of the items are referred with their translated name, but their japanese names is written in front.
